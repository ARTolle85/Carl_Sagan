A Quick Look At The Man

  • Born November 9th 1934
  • Attended the University of Chicago
  • Earned a Bachelor of Science in physics, 1955
  • Earned a Master of Science in physics, 1956
  • Earned his Doctor of Philosophy in astonomy and astrophyiscs, 1960
  • In 1968 started working at Cornell University, also working with NASA since its inception
  • TV series Cosmos came out 1980
  • Dies December 20th 1996
